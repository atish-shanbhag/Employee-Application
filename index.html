About

It requires awe-inspiring vision to transform a rather unknown temple town into a thriving educational township. This credit goes to Dr. Mohan Alva, the founder and chairman of Alva’s Education foundation®, because of whom Moodbidri has become an epitome of social engineering in modern India. Moodbidri, which is also known as Jain Kashi, is a busting town cuddled in the green Valleys of Western Ghats, 34kms from the coastal town of Mangalore.
